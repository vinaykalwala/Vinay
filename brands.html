TOP SPORTSWEAR BRANDS

When it comes to the top sports brands, an old football adage comes to mind: shape is transient, but class is everlasting. Popularity comes and goes, but quality and legacy stay. Financially, availability, not exclusivity, is critical for athletic firms. Limited editions and exclusive collaborations may show up in a lot of social media feeds and bring a new degree of appeal to a sports brand, but those trainers seldom end up in everyone's wardrobes. However, while widespread availability and visibility do equal cold, hard cash for sportswear brands, exclusivity and collaborations remain a form of soft power that every label wishes to wield.
