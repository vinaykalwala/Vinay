Solar energy uses

 

²  Photovoltaic for electricity

It is a nonpolluting, renewable, and inexhaustible energy. With help of solar energy photovoltaic cells convert sunlight into electricity. Solar cells also called photovoltaic cells, and some photovoltaic cells convert artificial light into electricity.

²  Passive solar heating for space heating, and cooling

 The design of passive solar space heating, and cooling system that uses solar energy.In this process of living spaces by exposure to the sun. When sunlight affects a building, building materials can reflect, transmit, and absorb solar radiation.

Solar heat transfers from warmer spaces to cooler spaces. In summertime inside houses, heat delivers to the outdoor environment, functioning like an air conditioner. For cooling strategic uses ventilation, windows, shade,  trees, etc produce a comfortable level in indoor spaces depending on the environmental condition.

²  Solar thermal power systems

Solar thermal power systems use solar energy. In this system, solar radiation is used to generate electricity, and it is used in the power plant process. In this process, solar water heats collect, and stored in tanks liquid collects heat direct from the sun.

 The heated liquid circulates through the exchanger in the tank, the heat transfer to the water and finally power is generated. This system is used in space heating, cooling, water heating, heat for process industries, and power generation.

²  Solar water heating 

In modern days some places solar water heaters are used in daily life, it is one of the easy ways to water heating, and they can provide hot water all day long whenever require it. In swimming pools water is steadily flowing from the pool through the solar heating panel, and back to the pool, when the water temperature rises in the pool.

The cost May be solar water heaters higher than traditional water heaters. In this process, solar energy benefits the environment without any electricity or other uses. Solar heaters are used depending on the seasons, climatic conditions, environmental conditions, etc.

Charging batteries with help of solar panels converting sunlight into DC electricity. With  Dc electricity, we can charge our batteries properly, and not damage them. This is a great way to charge your batteries with help of solar panels. This the clean, and renewable energy.
