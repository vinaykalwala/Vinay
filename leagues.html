BIGGEST SPORTS LEAGUES IN THE WORLD

The first competition, which lasted 44 days in 2008, was won by the Rajasthan Royals, a team from a lesser market, under the leadership of the legendary Australian bowler Shane Warne. Following the IPL's popularity, other cricketing nations raced to create their own domestic T20 tournaments in an effort to share in the wealth. With its inception in 2008, the tournament not only revolutionised the face of the game, but it is also one of the largest leagues in the entire athletic globe. According to Duff & Phelps, a consultancy firm, the IPL is currently one of the most valuable brands, with a brand value of $6.3 billion in 2018. The competition has not only revolutionised the game, but it has also had an impact on India's economy, adding $11.5 billion to the country's GDP in 2015.
