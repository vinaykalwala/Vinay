Progressive muscular relaxation

It's common to have anxiety from time to time. However, if your stress builds up or persists for an extended length of time, you may carry the tension in your muscles. You may be experiencing muscular stiffness without even recognizing it. Progressive muscular relaxation, often known as Jacobson's relaxation technique, is one method for relieving muscle tension. Progressive muscle relaxation (PMR) is a method of therapy that involves precisely contracting and releasing muscle units one at a time.

The Body Scan

Meditation is a practice that trains the mind to focus and redirect thoughts. It involves finding a quiet place, sitting comfortably, and either closing the eyes or focusing on an object or sound. The goal is to cultivate mental clarity, inner calmness, and awareness. By observing thoughts and emotions without judgment, meditation helps develop self-understanding. The regular practice offers benefits like reduced stress, improved concentration, increased self-awareness, emotional well-being, and inner peace. Meditation can be practiced in both spiritual and secular contexts, with different techniques available, such as mindfulness or loving-kindness meditation.
