What is Environment

The natural surrounding that all the organisms exist in is called the environment, the living and non-living components of the earth and how they interact with one another. Understanding the concept of the environment is highly essential because it helps us know and appreciates the importance of preserving the world we live in and the impact we have on it. The environment not just helps us exist, but provides us with various benefits such as ecosystem services, and resources for our survival and well-being. Even if one of the components of the environment is skipped, it may pose a threat to all living and non-living beings in one way or the other. This makes understanding the Environment more important to save it from any such potential dangers. This blog focuses on providing a deep understanding of what the environment requires, the importance of conserving it, and the responsibilities we have as individuals to ensure we safeguard it for future generations and invest in sustainability.
