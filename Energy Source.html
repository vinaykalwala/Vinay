SOLAR ENERGY AND USES

Solar energy is renewable energy, it comes from solar radiation or heat, or light. Solar energy play important role in reducing mitigation climate change, and greenhouse gas emissions. It is a natural energy source refilled at a higher rate than they consumed. There is no chance of pollution of the environment no pollution occurring while producing solar energy. Solar energy maintenance costs are very low, and it is environmentally friendly. It is a very important source of energy in the world. We can use solar energy in different ways in our daily life. In houses set up solar panels on the roof, and solar panels reduce our electricity bills. This is the best future source of energy, and it improves air quality and reduces water use from energy production. Solar energy is essential to protect humans, wildlife, and ecosystems.
