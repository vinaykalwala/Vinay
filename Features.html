Visualization

Meditation is a highly relaxing process that also helps in connection with one’s spiritual and higher self. Visualization is a meditation technique in which images, scenarios or feelings are visualized that provide immense peace and a sense of happiness and contentment. Many times, experiencing some feelings, being in some places or with someone is not possible but with visualisation, this can be felt and seen, helping in release of happy hormones, and hence helping in mental and emotional stability. Medidation is very helpful in achieving mindfulness, that is being mentally active in all situations. By picturing and experiencing positive feelings and places, it is possible to focus on the present moment, letting go of past and future anxieties. Visualization is a powerful tool that can be used in conjunction with other meditation techniques to improve mental and emotional well-being.
