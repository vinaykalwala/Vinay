GREY HAT HACKING AND IT’S MOTIVE

A hacker who falls in between the white hat and black hat classifications is known as a grey hat hacker. While Grey hat hackers occasionally reveal vulnerabilities to the affected parties or the entire public, they occasionally use vulnerabilities for their own personal advantage. There are exceptions to any generalization, which is why the idea of "Grey hat" hackers also exists. Hackers that use Grey hats fall in between "good" and "bad." They are the ones that examine a system for flaws without authorization but maybe with good intentions. They could let a company know that they have found a way to abuse their system and then demand payment to have it fixed.

WHITE HAT HACKING

A white hat hacker, also known as an ethical hacker, is someone who employs hacking talents to find security flaws in hardware, software, or networks. Unlike black hat hackers – or malevolent hackers – white hat hackers adhere to the rule of law when it comes to hacking. Many white hat hackers have previously worked as black hat hackers. The names are derived from classic Western films in which heroes wore white hats and villains wore black hats.

WHITE HAT SEO AND TECHNIQUES

White hat SEO refers to SEO techniques that adhere to the standards and guidelines set out by search engines and users. It comprises using methods to raise a website's position in search results without utilizing gimmicks to undermine a search engine's algorithm. White hat SEO tactics prioritize giving readers high-quality and pertinent material that improves user experience, as opposed to utilizing more spammy approaches like duplicating content that is exclusively optimized for search engines.
