TOP SPORTSWEAR BRANDS

 

About top sports brands

 

When it comes to the top sports brands, an old football adage comes to mind: shape is transient, but class is everlasting. Popularity comes and goes, but quality and legacy stay.

 

Financially, availability, not exclusivity, is critical for athletic firms. Limited editions and exclusive collaborations may show up in a lot of social media feeds and bring a new degree of appeal to a sports brand, but those trainers seldom end up in everyone's wardrobes. However, while widespread availability and visibility do equal cold, hard cash for sportswear brands, exclusivity and collaborations remain a form of soft power that every label wishes to wield.

 

The sportswear business is thriving and experiencing unprecedented growth, thanks to some of the world's biggest sportswear brands, which have maintained their popularity, performance, and sales figures in recent years. Nike and Adidas are the two brands that have maintained their dominance over decades.

 

Many sportswear brands and their product lines of performance sportswear, trainers, yoga apparel and sports apparel are well-known among athletes. They have managed to slip under people's radars and garner enormous appeal among the general public and the high fashion industry.

 

To learn more about some of the world's best athletic brands, we'll look at their product lines, labels, and collections.

 

The 10 Best Sportswear Brands in the world

 

1.Hoka

 

Origin Country  : Annecy, France

Founder : Jean-Luc Diard and Nicolas Mermoud

Founded : In 2009

Headquarters : Goleta, California

 

Hoka is a French sportswear company that specialises in maximalist running shoes and was created in 2009 by two former Salomon workers. It may not have the same tradition as other companies on our list due to its relatively brief existence, but it has acquired a solid footing in both the running business thanks to the engineering contained in its products. You might argue that the brand's success was mostly due to the chunky sneaker trend, which Fila also benefited from, but the numbers don't lie. HOKA specialises in developing trail-ready trainers, such as the Bondi X, which gained popularity due to the usage of an early stage meta-rocker, which offers a seamless transition whether walking or running.

 

2.ASICS

 

Origin Country: Japan

Founder: Kihachiro Onitsuka

Founded: 1949

Headquarters: Kobe, Japan

 

Few businesses combine performance and design as well as ASICS. Although it is best known for its impact in the running world with cutting-edge silhouettes such as the Novablast and Meta Speed Sky, its lifestyle models are well-known for their comfort. Almost every model from the brand's Gel series provides stability and cushioning in a manner that other brands do not, and with Kiko Kostandinov at the creative helm, the brand's extensive line of retro-runners is experiencing a renaissance.

 

3.Reebok

 

Origin Country: USA

Founder: Jeff Foster, Joe Foster

Founded: 1958

Headquarters: Boston, U.S.

 

One-time British heritage sportswear firm Reebok has dived into its vault and offered it up to a bevy of collaborators, including celebrities and brands such as Cardi B, Maison Margiela, and Rihanna.

 

Reebok has positioned itself as one of the top sports companies in the world, with classic versions from Allen Iverson's question and answer series giving new sneakerheads a chance to secure timeless styles. Reebok recently continued to pander to the footwear world by releasing the Beatnik, one of the few slippers designed for cold weather.

 

Along with the overhyped models, Reebok's general releases, such as the Club C, haven't slowed down, and the brand now offers trail-ready trainers as well as marathon runners.

 

Reebok entered the market as a sports shoe brand and is currently competing with leading sportswear brands such as Puma and Adidas. Reebok has established its own identity and is well-known for its high-quality sports equipment and clothes for both men and women. The brand assortment of Reebok sportswear comprises track jackets, track pants, purses, hats, sunglasses, shoes and flip-flops.

 

4.Puma

 

Origin Country: Herzogenaurach, Germany

Founder: Rudolf & Adolf Dassler

Founded: October 1,1948.

Headquarters: Herzogenaurach, Germany

 

Puma's revenues increased by almost 32% in 2021, making it one of the highest-earning years for the German sportswear company. Puma's success may be attributed to their commitment to providing high-quality items at competitive prices. Collaborations with Pokémon, MCM, and shop Butter Goods helped strengthen Puma outside of its hefty football basis, and while Neymar Jr. remains one of the brand's most prominent contracted sportsmen, younger stars in a variety of sports are following suit.

 

Though modern iterations of its popular Suede silhouette are still released every year, Puma's ability to stay true to its athletic roots is the reason why it remains one of the world's top five sportswear brands.

 

Puma is a German sports company that has quickly taken over the gym wear and sports market. Gym attire is offered for both men and women. The company is well-known in India for supporting major cricket, football, and golf teams. Puma's main store and other e-commerce websites sell sportswear. Puma is well-known for its high-quality products such as shoes, training kits, t-shirts, digital watches, and other accessories.

 

5.Converse

 

Origin Country: United States of America

Founder: Marquis Mills Converse

Founded: 1908

Headquarters: Boston, Massachusetts, U.S.

Annual Revenue in 2022: 2.346 billion USD

 

Converse is one of the original American sportswear companies that has continued to develop throughout the years. Founded in 1908, the company rose to prominence with its internationally recognised Chuck Taylor style, which has gone through various variations over the previous century. Though it's uncommon to see shoes transition between sports, Converse has redefined itself as a skate brand by adding a vulcanised sole to a few of its silhouettes, sponsoring young pros like Louis Lopez, Sage Elsesser, and 2019 Skater of the Year Milton Martinez. Though the brand isn't as active in basketball as it once was, it still has a presence on the court with players such as Warriors Draymond Green and Oklahoma City's Shai Gilgeous-Alexander.

 

6.Jordan Brand

 

Origin Country: United States

Founder: Peter Moore, Tinker Hatfield & Bruce Kilgore

Founded: September 9,1997.

Headquarters: Beaverton,Oregon

 

Many sportswear brands have shifted their focus to reviving and reimagining chunky runners from their heyday in the 1990s. Still, Jordan Brand produces remarkable retros on an entirely different level, all thanks to the iconic Air Jordan 1.

 

A separate Excel sheet would be required to include all of Jordan's partners over the years, although notables like Virgil Abloh, Hiroshi Fujiwara, Paris Saint-Germain F.C., and J Balvin have all made their imprint on the Jordan vintage line. Aside from classic reissues, Jordan Brand also sells modern basketball and football gear, and it recently signed its first pro-BMX athlete, Nigel Sylvester, for 2021.

 

The future appears promising. Jordan, along with young NBA players such as Luka Doncic and Jayson Tatum, performed atas the brand develops to collaborate with distinctive designers and retailers all around the world.

 

7.Le Sportif Coq

 

Origin Country: French

Founder: Emile Camuset

Founded: 1882

Headquarters: Entzheim, France

 

Tennis enthusiasts would associate the moniker Le Coq Sportif with tennis luminaries such as Yannick Noah and Arthur Ashe, who both used the brand's rooster insignia throughout their playing days.

 

But the storied French sports brand, founded in 1882, is much more than just tennis. In the 1980s, Le Coq Sportif became the kit supplier for a slew of football clubs, including the Aston Villa squad that won the European Cup in 1982, as well as the FIFA World Cup-winning teams of Italy in 1982 and, most importantly, Argentina in 1986. Maradona wore a Le Coq Sportif jersey when he scored his legendary "Hand of God" goal against England.

 

But that's not allabout the past, and Le Coq Sportif maintains its position as a seller of clean, no-nonsense sportswear. Collaborations have recently included the Scottish shoe company Hanon and the now-defunct, renowned colette.

 

8.Nike

 

Origin Country: Americon

Founder: Bill Bowerman, Phil Knight

Founded: 1964

Headquarters: Unicorporated Washing country near Beaverton, Oregon, U.S.

 

The Beaverton behemoth is the world's largest athletic brand, with more than $44 billion in revenue expected in 2021. Nike controls almost every sport, with sponsorships ranging from amateurs to Olympians, AAU teams to NBA teams, and everything in between. For decades, the Swoosh has been one of the world's most recognisable logos.

 

The brand's goods are always evolving, with releases ranging from the ZoomX Vaporfly Next% to its first cryptokicks release earlier this year.

 

Of course, coveted drops like Virgil's and Louis Vuitton's AF1 collaboration and Drake's NOCTA mark aided the brand's stranglehold on the luxury sportswear business, but Nike is just in a league of its own.

 

Nike is the world's most popular gym and sportswear company. The American brand is quite popular among gym goers and sportsmen. Nike has the greatest apparel for men and women. Nike's online store, as well as Amazon and other websites, sell sportswear. Nike sportswear items include sneakers, sandals, t-shirts, track trousers and sports accessories.

 

9.Under Armour

 

Origin Country: America

Founder: kevin plank

Founded: 1996

Headquarters: Baltimore, Maryland, U.S.

 

Under Armour is another American sportswear firm that specialises in footwear, athletic gear, and casual wear. Under Armour, headquartered in Baltimore, Maryland, has made tremendous strides as a premium sportswear manufacturer during the last decade. It is well-known for its collaboration with NBA superstar Stephen Curry. He may be described as the "face of their footwear line" in his own words. Under Armour has prospered in the sports industry owing to its consistent attempts to use sports technology to elevate the sportswear experience.

 

At CES in 2018, Under Armour debuted a pair of connected running shoes. Each shoe has a Bluetooth module, accelerator, and gyroscope. In addition, Under Armour is rumoured to be developing a new type of shirt called "Coldblack," which would reflect the heat and help keep athletes cool in the sun.

 

Based on popularity and global sportswear sales, Under Armour is placed fourth among the world's 15 greatest sportswear brands in 2022. To capture the lucrative footwear market, the sneaker business has signed Stephen Curry as its trademark athlete. It has also inked arrangements with other players, such as Tom Brady and Canelo Alvarez, to expand into other markets.

 

Under Armour is a well-known sports company that produces casual clothing, sportswear, and footwear. Athletic shoes, protective gear, caps, gloves, bags, athletic bags, athletic bras, shorts, leggings, pants, hoodies, jackets and t-shirts are all available. The sportswear company also produces uniforms for numerous sports such as American football, basketball, and soccer, among others.

 

10.Fila Holdings Corp

 

Origin Country: Italy

Founder: Ettore Fila, Giansevero Fila

Parent Company: Fila Korea Ltd

Founded: 1911

Headquarters: Biella, Piedmont, Italy

 

Fila Holdings Corp, an Italian corporation, is ranked ninth in the list of the best sportswear brands in 2022, based on popularity and worldwide sportswear sales. Following the acquisition of Fila Holdings by Fila Korea, the sports brand now considers South Korea to be its home and has launched a public offering on the South Korean Exchange.

 

Fila is a well-known lifestyle brand in the fashion industry. Its product offers include athletic shoes, sports equipment, and clothes, and it is affiliated with the sports goods and sportswear sectors.

 

In 2019, the company announced a collaboration with BTS, making it one of the historic athletic brands affiliated with the famed band.
