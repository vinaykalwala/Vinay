ENERGY SOURCES

In the world energy sources are on the rise. These energy sources are key to resisting climate change. Energy sources are used to perform our work. Once we used the energy sources some energy sources are restored again and again we can use how much we want, this type of energy source is called renewable energy source. Other sources are called nonrenewable. Nonrenewable when we use these energy sources there is no chance of producing these types of energy sources these are limited sources in the world. These energy sources are naturally available it may renewable or nonrenewable. Energy sources use like generating electricity, wind power, hydroelectric, etc.

Important of energy sources

     Energy sources are a foundation of complete production activity. Without energy sources, production activity cannot operate. It is very important in the development process. These sources are used large scale not only in the industrial sectors, agriculture sector, etc. Without energy sources, there is no life on the earth. Energy sources are important in life, and all living organisms. For all activities, energy is required. It is used in cooking, lighting, heating, growing crops for agriculture,  transportation, communication, and driving machines in industries all these kind of activities are possible only with the help of energy sources.

Energy sources are divided into 2 types

1. Renewable energy

2. Non Renewable energy sources

 Renewable energy

Nowadays importance of Renewable energy is becoming increasingly. These Renewable energy sources naturally occur. It is used in our daily life. Types of Renewable energy are solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and biomass. Solar energy is used for generating electricity, and with the help of wind we can generate wind power, and with the force of the moving water, we can create hydroelectric power. Harness heat from the earth's core geothermal energy will be created. Plants and animals help these types of organic materials we can use in our daily life because of our work. We all depend on renewable energy sources.

l  Solar Energy:

Solar energy is one type of energy generated by the sun. It is renewable energy. It will come from sun radiation. With help of solar energy, we can convert energy into power. With solar energy, we can save money. It satisfies all present and future needs. In modern days solar energy is used for light, heat, and electromagnetic radiation. Solar energy is commonly used in homes and businesses, and industry. How much we want we can use it.

l  Hydro Energy:

Hydro energy is one of the energy sources it will come from water. It is renewable energy. It will come from water. With the help waterfall electricity is generated. Hydro power is generated by huge dams, rivers, and other water bodies. Hydro power energy is used for our needs in the world like electricity power supply to homes, industrious, for business purposes. The benefits of hydro power are to control floods, irrigation support, and low-cost electricity.

l  Wind energy:

Wind energy is a natural source. It will come from air or wind, wind energy is renewable energy. With help of wind energy, we can generate mechanical power or electricity. Huge turbines convert wind energy into mechanical power. This mechanical power is used in grinding grain or pumping water, and electricity. The turbines are placed in rounded hills and open plains. Force of the wind will be generated. In the world, most people follow this process.

l  Geothermal  energy:

Geothermal energy is renewable energy. It will take from the earth's core. This energy is stored in rocks and fluids in the center of the earth. In geothermal energy heat energy comes from the earth. Geothermal energy can use in different ways in present resources and technology to cool, heat, and create electricity for present and future needs.

l  Biomass energy:

Biomass energy is renewable energy. It comes from organic materials like plants, animals, and other materials. Biomass energy stores chemical energy from the sun. It will burn heat that comes directly to the body, and it is converted to either maybe liquid, gases,  or fuels in a different process. It is used to produce electricity heating water, and building, in industrious used for heat, and create electricity in-stream turbines.

l  Tidal energy:

Tidal energy is renewable energy. The power is produced from the natural rise, and fall of currents. In this process of technology turbines, and paddles are used. Tidal energy is created by the ocean waters at the time of the rise and fall of tides. In the tidal system turbines placed underwater in a stream, it creates energy to give electricity to our homes and business. This energy in some places is used alternately to burn coal, and oil which provides to global warming.

l  Ocean energy:

Ocean energy is also renewable energy. It is used three main technology wave, ocean thermal, and tidal. Compared three technology wave energy is more costly. Because of this energy electricity is produced from the movement of waves, tides, and ocean currents. Energy obtains from the ocean’s movement or form of chemical or physical state. This energy stored underwater compresses air energy and water-pumped hydro storage. Ocean energy is used for coastal communication, underwater robots, remote islands, for marine research, fishing, and military operations.

Nonrenewable energy sources

Nonrenewable sources once we use them will not restore again and again. It will decrease while we used it for our work, and there is no chance to restore these types of nonrenewable energy sources. Nonrenewable energy sources like Coal, natural gas, oil, and clean energy. Our all world depends on these nonrenewable energy sources in our daily life. These types of energy sources also came from nature.

Ø  Fossil fuels:

Fossil fuels are nonrenewable energy. Fossil fuels like Oil, natural gas, coal, peat, etc.It will be formed because of decomposed dead animals, and plants that body's in underground for millions of years. Some Fossil fuels come from volcanic eruptions etc. It is used to create energy for our needs like large power stations, electricity, and powerful engines. Once we used more fossil fuels we can’t restore.

Ø  Coal:

Coal is a nonrenewable resource of energy. The formation of coal takes millions of years. Coal will be formed from dead organic materials like animals and plants converted like coal because of environmental conditions, and geological forces of heat and pressure it will take millions of years. Uses of coal cement production, medicines, tars, petroleum fuels, in homes commercial heating, electricity generation, etc.

Ø  Crude oils:

Crude oils formed from dead organisms like algae, and zooplankton exists millions of years ago in marines. Many millions of years ago above the dead organisms some layers were covered by sand, silt, and rock, because of heat and pressure it to convey crude oil or petroleum. These are comes from underground from the earth. Without crude oils, there is no life in the present and future generation why because these oils plays important role in modern days. We use oils for vehicles,  heat buildings, to produce electricity and feed stocks for making chemicals. If the use of these will increases we can’t restore them again.

Ø  Natural gas:

Natural gases are also nonrenewable sources same as oils. It comes from decomposed organic matter from millions of ancient years. Natural gases are the organic materials mixed with mud, silt, and sand on the sea floor, and they become buried over time. In industries natural gases are used to heat, produce chemicals, fertilized, power systems, and plant fuel.

Ø  Nuclear energy:

Nuclear energy is nonrenewable energy. It will forms from the nucleus, the core of atoms, made from protons, and neutrons. Nuclear energy comes from uranium elements, and it is mined from the ground available in certain locations. This energy is used for space exploration, nuclear energy for electricity, medical diagnosis treatment, criminal investigations, agriculture, etc. Nuclear energy is produced in two ways 1. Nuclear fission and,2.Nuclear fusion

1. Nuclear fission:

Nuclear fission when neutrons attack a large atom, and break the atom into two smaller atoms. In the process of breaking atoms, it will release energy. Power plants heat water to create steam. This steam is used to spin large turbines to produce electricity. The breaking of uranium has 235 nuclei when it is blasted with neutrons. Nuclear fission is used to create electricity, but first, it should be released from the atom. Nuclear reactors or power plant machines can control nuclear fission to create electricity.

2. Nuclear fusion:

Nuclear fusion is the reaction between the sun and other stars. In this reaction, two light nuclei merge to form a single large nucleus. This procedure releases energy because the total mass of the single nucleus is less than the mass of two original nuclei. It offers a long-term energy source, and it does not cause greenhouse or long-lived radioactive misuse. It is used for nuclear weapons before how they don’t use. Present the world nuclear fusion is used for power and electricity generation.
