greenhouse effect

Earth has not been the same as a few decades earlier. It did not use to be so hot, polluted, or devoid of rain. But now, the environment has become hotter, polluted everywhere, and the rain cycle has changed to the extreme. The root of all these problems is Global Warming which causes a temperature rise, which is caused by the Greenhouse effect. The greenhouse effect is called so because it imitates a glass greenhouse that is used to flourish plants in cold environments. A greenhouse traps the heat within, using certain atmospheric gases. This heat saves the plants from the harsh cold and helps them survive in lower temperatures just as they would do in normal temperatures.
